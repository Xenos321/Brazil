History

Brazil was colonized by Portugal in 1500 and gained independence in 1822. It has grown into a diverse and vibrant republic.

Map

Landmass

Brazil is the fifth-largest country in the world by area, covering 8.5 million square kilometers.

Population

Brazil has a population of over 213 million people, making it the sixth most populous country in the world.

Government

Brazil is a federal presidential constitutional republic.

Religion

Brazil is predominantly Christian, with Roman Catholicism being the largest denomination.
